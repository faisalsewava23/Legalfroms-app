Frequently asked questions

How do I install LegalForms on my phone?

Open legalformsapp.co.uk in Safari on iPhone or Chrome on Android. Tap the share icon and select Add to Home Screen. The app will appear on your home screen like a native app — no App Store download needed.

Which forms does LegalForms cover?

LegalForms covers all three attendance note types: Police Station (custody attendance), Magistrates Court, and Crown Court. Each form generates a professional PDF with your firm name at the top.

How does voice dictation work?

Tap the microphone button in any text field, speak your notes clearly, then tap to stop. Your speech is transcribed instantly using OpenAI Whisper, which is optimised for legal terminology. The transcribed text is inserted directly into the form field.

How do I generate and share a PDF?

Once you have completed your form, tap Generate PDF. Your attendance note is created instantly and can be saved to your device, shared via email, AirDrop, or any other app on your phone.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel at any time from within the app settings or by emailing us at hello@legalformsapp.co.uk. There are no cancellation fees and no contracts.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Audio sent for transcription is processed by OpenAI Whisper and is not stored after transcription. We do not sell or share your data with any third parties. See our Terms & Conditions for full details.

I found a bug — how do I report it?

Please email hello@legalformsapp.co.uk with a description of the issue, which device and browser you are using, and any steps to reproduce it. We take all bug reports seriously and aim to fix issues quickly.
